| Feature | Command/Action | Action Key | Credits | Notes |
|---------|---------------|------------|---------|-------|
| **Main Menu** | `menu`, `hi`, `hello` | `menu_navigation` | **1 credit = 2 commands** | Bundled with other commands |
| **Help** | `help` | `help_command` | **1 credit = 2 commands** | Bundled with other commands |
| **Check Balance** | `credits` | `check_balance` | **1 credit = 2 commands** | Bundled with other commands |
| **View Stats** | `stats` | `settings_access` | **1 credit = 2 commands** | Bundled with other commands |
| **Registration** | Registration flow | `registration_step` | **1 credit = 2 commands** | Bundled with other commands |

**How Bundling Works:**
- First command: No credit deducted (tracked)
- Second command: 1 credit deducted (bundle complete)
- New bundle starts after completion
